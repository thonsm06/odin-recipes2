Hamburger

Description

Juicy ground beef patty in between two halves of buns and cheese.

Ingredients

  • Ground Beef
  • Buns
  • American Cheese
  1. Form patty with ground beef
  2. cook ground beef to at least 160℉
  3. Add cheese to the patty near the end of cooking.
  4. Layer the cheesed patty onto the bottom bun then top bun.
