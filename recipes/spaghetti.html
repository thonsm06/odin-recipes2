Spaghetti

Description

Impress your guest with this italian classic.

Ingredients

  • Ground Beef
  • Red Sauce
  • Spaghetti Noodles
  • Parmesan Cheese
  1. Brown the beef in a sauce pan, drain part of grease or not is optional.
  2. Add sauce to the cooked beef and season to taste.
  3. Cook pasta in salted boiling water until aldente.
  4. Plate the noodle with sauce topping and finish with Parmesan cheese.
