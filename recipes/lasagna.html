Lasagna

Description

Seven layers of heaven with this lasagna recipe.

Ingredients

  • Ground Beef
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cheese
  • Red Sauce
  1. Brown the ground beef
  2. On a baking dish, layer sauce, then noodle, then cheese.
  3. Repeat all the way to the top of the dish.
  4. Bake at 350℉ for 1 hour, broil the last few minutes to brown the cheese.
